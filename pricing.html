Damage Deposit

A flat $100 security deposit is required for all rentals. This is fully refunded within 48 hours of gear return, provided equipment is in good condition.

Late Returns

Returns more than 4 hours late will incur a partial day charge. Returns over 24 hours late are charged at 1.5x the daily rate to cover logistics.

Cancellation Policy

Cancel up to 48 hours before pickup for a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours incur a 20% restocking fee.
