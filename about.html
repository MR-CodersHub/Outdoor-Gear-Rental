Core Objective

Democratizing The Great Outdoors.

At Peak Rental, we believe that high-quality gear shouldn't be a barrier to experiencing the great outdoors. Our mission is to provide professional-grade equipment that empowers everyone to explore further, stay longer, and discover the transformative power of nature.

We curate only the best brands, maintain them to the highest standards, and offer them at a fraction of the retail cost.

Chronology

Journey Timeline

2015

The Beginning

Founded by two avid hikers with just 5 tents and a passion for the trail. The vision was clear: high-performance gear accessible to all.

2018

Expansion

Opened our first physical basecamp and expanded into technical backpacks and sleep systems.

2021

Going National

Launched our trailhead delivery service across 15 national parks, redefining convenience for the modern explorer.

2024

Peak Sustainability

Achieved carbon-neutral status and launched our gear repair program. Engineering the future while protecting the present.
